Our Product

Our team developed an intuitive and easy-to-use automatic dispensing solution to fill this void in the market. Two main subassemblies, housing the electronics and condiments respectively are present, connected with threaded connection. To dispense, the user simply enters their desired condiment and volume in the interactive UI, and a stepper motor would rotate the correct channel to alignment. Following this, a servo would depress the channel and activate the corresponding valve for dispensing.
